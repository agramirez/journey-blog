## Formalism

The following is a formal definition of the goals and objectives of the SafeKatChat project.

As a high level overview of our goals, the SafeKatChat should;

- Have a web based interface for system administrators
- Have a web based interface for healthcare providers
- Have a web based interface for medical practice staff
- Have a web based interface for patients

It should provide the following set of functionality;

System Administrators
- Monitor the status of all the tech components
- Modify and configure default settings
- Add, edit, remove users 

Heathcare Providers
- Schedule meetings with patients
- Attend meetings with patients
- View recordings of past meetings with patients, if patients allow
- Annotate patient meetings
- Create, update, review notes

Medical Practice Staff
- Schedule,reschedule,cancel meetings between healthcare providers and patients
- View billable hours and meeting times
- Create, print, or email bills to patients
- Create and transmit bills to insurance companies
- Add, edit, remove patients

Patients
- Register for the service
- Access their meetings
- Send and receive messages
- Manage access of their data (allow/disallow )
- Manage their billing
    - View current and past bills
    - Add/edit/remove their insurance information
    - Pay their bill
